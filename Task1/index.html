Tribute to A.P.J.Abdul Kalam




About A.P.J Kalam


A.P.J Kalam, the "Missile Man of India", was born on October 15, 1931 in Raweswarram, Tamil Naddu. He served as the 11th President of India and was a renowned scientist, known for his work in India's space and Missile programs.


Acheivements

  • Developed India's Satellite launch vehicle.
  • Led India's succesful nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Wrote books "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020".
  • Received the Bharat Ratna, India's Highest civilian awards.
